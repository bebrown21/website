About Me

I earned my degree in Electrical Engineering from Tennessee Technological University, inspired by my father, a Nuclear Engineer. However, my true calling revealed itself years later when I discovered a profound passion for problem-solving through software. Fueled by this newfound interest, I ventured into the realm of self-taught software engineering, specializing in web development.

My enthusiasm for continual learning extends to both technological advancements and leadership skills. Armed with robust relational abilities, I boast a diverse background encompassing web development, leadership, teaching, mentoring, and expertise in Agile web development.
